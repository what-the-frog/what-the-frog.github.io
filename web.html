I was a developer before I even knew what that was
 
 
When I was about 12 years old I was introduced to the wonders of the world wide web. I quickly learned how to read HTML and figure out what part of the code did what by simply changing small parts of the code and looking at the results. This is pretty similar to the top down approach in genetics and how the genome of Drosophila melanogaster was mapped.

Learning by doing has always been my approach and I taught myself all there is to know a lot about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP.
